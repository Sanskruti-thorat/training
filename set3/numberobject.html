• Number.MAX_VALUE :Represents the largest positive finite numeric value.
  • Number.MIN_VALUE: Represents the smallest positive numeric value greater than zero.
  • Number.POSITIVE_INFINIY :Represents positive infinity.
  • Number.NEGATIVE_INFINITY:Represents negative infinity.
  • Number.NaN:Represents a value that is "Not-a-Number" (NaN).
  • Number.EPSILON:Represents the difference between 1 and the smallest value greater than 1 that can be represented as a Number.
