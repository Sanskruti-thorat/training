Welcome to MediCare

A state-of-the art modern facility in the heart of the Goa state, it is spread over 10 acres and has a built-up area of over 440000 square feet. Currently the hospital has 300+ beds with a capacity to expand to 400 beds. MediCare Hospital, GOA is a tertiary care flagship unit of the MediCare Hospitals Group.The Hospital focuses on centres of excellence like Cardiac Sciences, Neuro Sciences, Orthopaedics including Knee Replacement, Hip Replacement and Spine Surgery, Cancer, Emergency Medicine and Solid Organ Transplants besides the complete range of more than 35 allied medical disciplines under the same roof. MediCare Hospital, GOA provides holistic healthcare that includes prevention, treatment, rehabilitation and health education for patients, their families and clients by touching their lives.
